Regina is the capital of the Spinward Marches and one of the most important worlds in the Domain of Deneb. Its class-A starport handles the majority of trade traffic moving Spinward, and the world hosts the sector-level Imperial Navy and Scout Service headquarters.

## Characteristics

Regina has a dense, breathable atmosphere and extensive hydrosphere covering roughly 80% of the surface. A population in the billions supports a highly complex government and strict law levels enforced by Imperial marines and local constabulary.

## Points of Interest

- **Regina Starport (Class A)** — among the finest port facilities in the sector; repair, refit, and fuel available
- **Imperial Naval Base** — home to the 208th Fleet
- **Scout Service Way Station** — regional coordination hub for exploration and courier routes

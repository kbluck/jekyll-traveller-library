Regina Subsector (subsector C of the Spinward Marches) is the most populous and economically significant subsector in the sector. It contains the sector capital, Regina, as well as major Imperial Navy and Scout bases. The subsector sits at the junction of major trade routes connecting the Imperium to the Spinward frontier.

Anya Voss is a veteran free trader operating out of Regina Starport. She commands the 400-ton Far Trader *Meridian Cross* and is known for moving cargo through restricted subsectors.

## Background

Born on Terra and raised on colony transports, Voss has spent her entire adult life in deep space.
